Beyond what you know

Have you lately thought about you being included in behavioural tables is purely artifficial?

The truth is that people, taken one by one, ar not hundred percent profound/superfficial, analytical/instinctive, effusive/restrained. They are - rocket scientists and ticket controllers equally included - unique, incompareable and unrepeatable.

Each and every individual acts - and reacts – depending on a specific context, based on a difuse fusion of inferences and emotions. This blend is hard to compute and almost impossible to „pigeonhole”.

The only reasonably acceptable statement would be that the nowadays humans are fundementally situational. Regardless his or her age, education, social status and influential circles. In fact, all these traits, along with many other factors, irrevocably define each individual’s personality.

Holding an affiliation card to „Homo contextualis” hyperclass is what you’re having in common with the ones around you.

Contemporary individuals are positioned themselves - or positioned by others, against their own will - in unique contexts, at different times. The problem is that he or she does not always succeed in defining those contexts. Which come one after another, assembling together his or her own existence.

Therefore, prior to any self-evaluation process, the first step is paramount. It consists in defining the personal context.

Beyond what I know

I have to confess: I find not easy at all to describe myself. That is not because I wouldn’t have a lot to tell about my experience as a coaching practitioner. But instead of talking, I usually choose to actually be there, to do things that the others will remember of.

I also have to admit: I am not, and never have been, an easy handling one. I think about what I’m saying and I say what I am thinking of. Out of respect for the one I’m talking to. Even when my words are not exactly those who the other wants to hear. I take that risk.

I am a perfectionist, a comprehensive and fair keen-observer, critical primarily with myself. I don’t choose the easy way in finding solutions. I rather look for the paths that give me confidence in perspectives. Or the perspective of assurance.

I hardly obey an authority, unless behind it there are individuals I appraise and whose actions prove their value winthin out-of-the ordinary contexts.

I trully believe in fair-play and I stand on defensive when someone is trying to sweep away a rival by unequal weaponery or by unfair means.

I have the solid conviction that each individual is unique, incomparable and unreapeatable, therefore I indulge myself to hope that the experiences I will share with any of them will be alike.

ONE TO ONE

Watching in a different light

„My own life” is a tag that lost through recent times both its atributes of differentiation and the inner power of singularity. One of the causes of this process is each one’s positioning in a „frame of reference”.

No one can live with the feelling of having everything. Nor with the impression of being easy to give up everything. That’s because one cannot belong to himself or herself hundred percent. The impact that everyone around you has upon your own being may sometimes lead to trublesome consequences: lack of accuracy and effectiveness in communication, futile regrets, less intense aspirations, painfully breakups and so on. The opposite way to look around is nevertheless sustainable. You may also have your own role, sometimes compelling, over the ones you relate with. The „frames of reference” multiply themselves, become layers, break apart and rejoin in different shapes.

„Homo Contextualis” has problems that cannot be solved by building understructures made out of alikeness. Your dilemas and states of anguish will never be identical with someone else’s. Consequently, your set of solutions to wipe out will never be the same with someone else’s. When you find yourself in a tight corner, you need someone able to take a step back, in order to keep an eye on and objectively evaluate your self-context.

Between you and me...

I have never met so far someone to simultaneously be an actor in his or hers play and a spectator of it. „Homo Conextualis” encounters barriers in evaluating a contex and finding the right solution when trying to duplicate.

Today, we can find the best keys for removing these obstacles in a co-op formula: sportsman-coach, candidate – mentor, attendant – master. aspirant-îndrumător, discipol-maestru. The The relationship’s potentiality grows when the two of them proclaim to be equal on every account.

The inner power of the coaching process resides in the space created between the willing to change and the fear that the change would never happen. „I am the best , but you are the best too.” is the credo which I share with my client whenever I start a new coaching session.

No one knows better his or hers past, attempts, failures, emotions, thoughts and feelings than the person in front of me. He or she is the one to take decisions for the future. I support, from outside the personal frame, the effort to define the context.

CORPORATE

Inside-out

Apparently, the easiest definition for „Homo Conextualis” to phrase is the one reffering to its own professional biosphere.

To define – yes. But not to evaluate. Even in large organisations, or especially within them, the hierarchies, positions and compatibilities are somehow well-established. Or they should be. At least on the surface.

Today, in almost every corporate structure, there is an almost cinical rule which reveals a tough inner reality: „The graveyards are full of indispensable men”. Regardless your position within an hierarchy, the horizontal and vertical inter-connectivity may bring into settings in which both personal and professional relations, together with your fulfillment, may be affected.

An organisation’s positioning on a collective marketplace might be easy to outline. To put into practice principles that lead to profficent results by eliminating malfunctions – this process could be harder.

Defining the context, both individual and as part of a group, becomes crucial. Taking a step back becomes a must.

Outside-in

The teams, the jobs, horizontally and vertically built relationships, professional responsabilities, even the coffee breaks gossip – all these have complex attributes, sometimes hard to deal with. Because – remember? – no context is identical to another. Finding the access key to humans’ and organistaions’ resources implies expertise and the ability to see the whole picture.

The main objective I follow when my help and expertise are called for is to make the teeams, and the people who make the teams to dvelop their capacity to solve the problems are confronted with by themselves.

Finding the path to focus on, gathering relevant information, putting the finger on common likes and dislikes pinpoiting the details, are some of my main priorities when working with a team.

I never forget to bring to the surface topics that are meant not to be discussed. By doing this, I find unexpected, creative in order to redifine an entire setup. The dynamics of a team are of a high relevance in evaluating a team’s emotional adequacy, which may not always be positive.

PARENTING

Got time?

One of the most terrible enemies confronting nowadays parents is time.

They don’t search, as Proust did, for their lost time. They don’t want just to regain. They want even more. They want to inflate the days, the hours, even the minutes spent together with the ones they gave birth to.

A reality hard to swallow for „Homo Contextualis” is that she or he doesn’t anymore find the time – as a unit of measurement, but also as an emotional dimension – for her or his own children.

The individual contexts, especially the proffessional ones, undermine their resources of lateral, creative thinking, which they wish to share.

The sequel? A child may witness parents’ dissatisfactions, sorrows or outbreaks. A child may put a self-embargo on his or hers natural will to apprehend, to assimilate, sometimes to perform.

Deep-seated values

It might sound like a clichee, but no one stepped into life as a parent for children, but bearing the condition of a child for his or her parents. The way a mother and a father behave within their daily routine, the values that guide them, their aspirations or their unconfessed bitterness – all these become once and for all marks for their kids or teens. And they will precisely and inevitable influence their destinies.

It’s not easy at all to manage a „proffesional” relationship with a pampered, bumpy, or vulnerable child. Nor with parents overloaded with responsabilities. Frustred by their lack of time for their own children.

I have the profound conviction that such contexts can be changed and improved. As long as we are willingly to frame a team. In which to act as equals. Our mature experiences confirm the fact that some childs suffer from the fear of exposure, which inhibits communication and, consequently, diminishes the longing for a change.

Hopefully, we will always have a three-pillared relationship. And we will always pick up together the place and the time period that will give to all the involved full confidence.
