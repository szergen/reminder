COACHING & TRAINING

to be aware or not to be aware

BEYOND WHAT YOU KNOW

Each individual acts (and reacts) according to their context. This way, we position ourselves somewhere between reason and emotion. This diffuse amalgam is difficult to quantify and almost impossible to classify into mass profiles.
Thus, the contemporary individual is essentially a situational human being in that age, education, status and the spheres of influence are the factors that leave their mark on our unique personality.

The fact that contemporary individuals belong to the ‘Homo Contextualis’ class places us (whether we are willing to accept it or not) in unique contexts at different stages of our lives.
However, we can’t always define these sets of circumstances, which make up our existence, one by one. And that is exactly the obstacle. Consequently, we can’t move on without defining our context. This is why when we are at a crossroads and we come across competent professional assistance, we can’t help but shout: ‘Eureka!’

BEYOND WHAT I KNOW

I strongly believe that each and every one of us is unique. And that is why I will allow myself to hope that the experiences we are going to share will be as unique as our personality.
I think when I speak and I speak what I think and that is out of respect for my interlocutor.
My best friends are: hard work, not forgetting that there is a human being in front of me and not just a case, my passion for the things that I really believe in and the curiosity about each individual’s inner world.

I appreciate well-organized work and counteracting superficiality by paying attention to details. Instead of talking about me, I would rather stand by you, get involved and do things that you will remember and that will turn into happy memories.

CORPORATE

From team player to coach

In whatever situation we may find ourselves in, it is important to define our own context and take a step back.
And if we compare our activity to a team sport, then the sportspeople need a coach in both trying times and moments of glory.


If we were to have the big picture and the corresponding training, we could use people’s resources and those of their organisations in order to overcome different obstacles.
This way, we could further develop the necessary skills to come up with solutions. The dynamics of a team provides precious information on the emotional potential of its members. PARENTS AND CHILDREN

AND WHAT ABOUT OUR TIME?

Time is one of the fiercest enemies parents have nowadays.
They want to prolong the moments they spend with their offspring, wishing for more quality time.

Parents no longer succeed in making time for their children, either from a temporal or an emotional standpoint.
Their individual contexts, especially the professional ones, undermine their ability to concentrate on the big picture and their creative thinking in those situations they are dealing with.
As a consequence, both parents and children may inhibit their natural wish to know more, to express themselves through art, to assimilate new things and grow.

Everything that happens with a child at a given time and place is deeply influenced by everything that occurs before...and afterwards.

    • find solutions
    • use two powerful tools: your thoughts and words to reshape reality

This approach gives you time for reflection; the process is a short-term one and it has a significant impact.

AND WHAT ABOUT OUR TIME?

Time is one of the fiercest enemies parents have nowadays. They want to prolong the moments they spend with their offspring, wishing for more quality time.

Parents no longer succeed in making time for their children, either from a temporal or an emotional standpoint. Their individual contexts, especially the professional ones, undermine their ability to concentrate on the big picture and their creative thinking in those situations they are dealing with. As a consequence, both parents and children may inhibit their natural wish to know more, to express themselves through art, to assimilate new things and grow.

Everything that happens with a child at a given time and place is deeply influenced by everything that occurs before...and afterwards.

All parents have started their lives being children and everything they have accumulated may leave its mark on their offspring. In the coaching sessions through art, my guiding and fundamental principle is teamwork in a trilateral relationship based on equality: child-parent-coach. First and foremost, we will have a meeting in which we assess everybody’s needs, we understand what we want to accomplish and we draw up a plan of action together.
